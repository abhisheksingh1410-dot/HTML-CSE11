"THIS IS SEMENTIC TAGS(header,footer,h1-h6,section, article,nav...)"

Semantic HTML tags clearly describe the purpose and meaning of the content they contain to both the browser and the developer. They provide structural context and enhance accessibility and SEO.

"THIS IS NON SEMENTIC TAGS (div,span...)"
Non-semantic HTML tags do not convey any specific meaning about the content they contain. They are primarily used for styling or grouping elements when no suitable semantic element exists.
